Organizational Setup

Define your organizational structure with plants, departments and all of your organization's hierarchies. Configure essential business masters that drive your operations. Set up approval workflows and role-based permissions that work across all operations.

Customer Relationship Management

Forecast demand and manage customer orders and schedules with production and procurement plan integration and order tracking.

Access sales analytics and reports to drive decisions.

Procurement and Inventory Management

Manage vendor onboarding and assessment. Create requisitions and automate purchase workflows. Generate material requirements through MRP. Handle inwarding, outsourced processing and inventory control. Access analytics with automated alerts.

Production

Define BOM and routing with production lines, cells and processes. Plan production from customer demand with material scheduling. Book production and manage stock adjustments. Access analytics with automated alerts for deviations.

Quality Management

Define and implement incoming, in-process and final inspection plans. Manage rejections, holds and non-conforming parts. Track vendor performance and handle customer returns. Access quality analytics with automated alerts for deviations.

Asset Management and Maintenance

Manage asset lifecycle from procurement to disposal. Schedule and manage autonomous and preventive maintenance. Manage breakdown workflows from reporting to restoration. Access analytics on machine health, availability and losses.

Business Excellence

Manage IMS documents with version control and approvals. Manage assessments and audits with maturity based models. Track continuous improvement projects and suggestions. Manage safety incidents with reporting and closure. Handle problem bank for quality, delivery and availability. Initiate and track CAPA systematically.

Accounts

Manage vendor payments and advances to streamline cash flow. Track customer payments to support collections. Access analytics and reporting on accounts payable and receivable to support financial decisions.

HR

Centralize employee information from personal details to login activation. Manage employee profiles, contact details and credentials.
