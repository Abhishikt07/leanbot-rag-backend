Asset Management Module

The Asset Management Software provides organizations with complete visibility and control over their physical assets. From acquisition to disposal, the software ensures that every asset is tracked, utilized optimally, and maintained throughout its lifecycle.


Add New Asset

Register new assets into the system with all key details (type, make, model, purchase details, vendor, pictures, etc).

  • Supports classification into production equipment, tools, machinery
  • Enables tagging for unique identification
  • Complete documentation with images

Asset Spare List

Maintain a structured database of spares and consumables linked to specific assets with document upload.

  • Track installed quantity
  • Monitor life span of spares
  • Preventive maintenance planning
  • Reduces downtime effectively

Asset Allocation

Allocate assets to plant, departments, section and line with display code for traceability.

  • Real-time status tracking
  • Performance monitoring
  • Optimizes asset utilization
  • Complete allocation history

Warranty Management

Store and track warranty details for each asset with complete history of warranty and service contract renewals.

  • Tracks warranty periods
  • Service contract renewals
  • Vendor coordination
  • Warranty claims management

Asset Life Cycle

Tracks rated capacity and working capacity with real-time status updates throughout asset lifecycle.

  • Performance history tracking
  • Depreciation monitoring
  • Utilization trend analysis
  • Financial audit compliance

Asset Exit & Upgradation

Streamlines approval workflow for asset disposal and upgradation with complete transparency.

  • Exit approval workflows
  • Upgradation requests
  • Investment decision support
  • Complete audit trail

Maintenance Management Module

Master the four pillars of maintenance to transform operations, reduce downtime, and maximize equipment reliability.


Breakdown Maintenance (BD)

Rapid reporting, response, and closure workflow when failures occur with real-time tracking system.

  • Rapid breakdown reporting system
  • Red Tag tracking with real-time visibility
  • Breakdown closure board
  • Improves team coordination
  • Enables root-cause analysis
  • Helps reduce downtime

Preventive Maintenance (PM)

Structured scheduling and tracking to avoid breakdowns before they happen with time-based maintenance plans.

  • Structured PM plans with checkpoints
  • Time-Based Maintenance (TBM) board
  • Real-time tracking and alerts
  • Avoids costly unplanned downtime
  • Ensure system compliance
  • Helps increase MTBF

Autonomous Maintenance (AM)

Empowering operators to perform routine checks and report abnormalities through CLIT framework - Cleaning, Lubrication, Inspection, and Tightening.

  • CLIT-based maintenance checklists
  • Abnormality reporting system
  • White/Yellow tag management
  • Establishes ownership at shop-floor level
  • Prevents minor issues from becoming breakdowns
  • Strengthens TPM implementation

Corrective Maintenance (CM)

Systematic improvements based on lessons learned, ensuring recurring issues are permanently resolved.

  • Captures recurring problem patterns
  • Tracks costs and effort
  • Links back to PM plans
  • Eliminates repeat failures permanently
  • Supports continuous improvement
  • Provides measurable ROI
